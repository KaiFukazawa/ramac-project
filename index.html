TL;DR

RAMAC couples an expressive diffusion actor with a distributional critic to learn risk-aware policies from offline data — achieving strong CVaR performance without sacrificing multimodality or incurring out-of-distribution actions.

Overview

RAMAC learns expressive multimodal offline RL policies while reducing unsupported action drift and catastrophic lower-tail outcomes by combining behavior regularization with CVaR optimization.

The framework couples a generative actor with a distributional critic, allowing risk-aware policy improvement without discarding the multimodal structure needed for challenging offline control settings.
